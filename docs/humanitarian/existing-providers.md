# Existing Humanitarian Providers

GeoResponde already integrates (or is actively working on integrating) the following humanitarian data providers.

Before researching new organizations, please review this list to avoid duplicates.

If you find a provider listed here, there is no need to include it in your research unless you have significant new information about it.

| Provider | Country | Primary Information |

| Venezuela Te Busca | Venezuela | Missing persons |

## What we're looking for

We are especially interested in providers that are **not already listed above**.

Potential examples include:

- NGOs
- Government emergency agencies
- Red Cross organizations
- Civil protection agencies
- Universities
- Humanitarian mapping initiatives
- Volunteer coordination platforms
- Shelter directories
- Food distribution networks
- Medical assistance directories
- Missing persons registries
- Road status services
- Utility outage maps
- Animal rescue organizations

Both Venezuelan and international organizations are welcome if they provide information that could be useful during disasters.
