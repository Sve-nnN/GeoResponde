# Humanitarian Provider Research Priorities

This document describes the types of humanitarian information providers currently prioritized by GeoResponde.

These categories are intended to guide research efforts and may evolve over time.

## Priority Categories

### Missing Persons

Platforms that help reunite families or report missing and found people.

### Shelters

Temporary shelters and evacuation centers.

### Hospitals

Hospital availability, admissions, emergency services and medical facilities.

### Medical Assistance

Medical brigades, emergency healthcare, pharmacies and first aid resources.

### Emergency Alerts

Official emergency notifications and warning systems.

### Damage Assessment

Building damage, infrastructure status and affected areas.

### Humanitarian Aid

Food distribution, water, medicine and essential supplies.

### Volunteer Coordination

Volunteer registration and community response initiatives.

### Civil Protection

Government emergency management agencies.

### NGOs

National and international humanitarian organizations.

### Crisis Mapping

Interactive emergency maps and crowdsourced mapping initiatives.

### Transportation

Road closures, bridge status and transportation updates.

### Utilities

Power outages, water service and telecommunications status.

### Animal Rescue

Organizations supporting lost pets and animal rescue during emergencies.

## Research Goal

The current objective is to identify **at least 20 additional providers** that are **not already included** in GeoResponde's existing provider catalog.

Before documenting a provider, please review `existing-providers.md` to avoid duplicate work.
